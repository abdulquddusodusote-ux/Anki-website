Help & User Guide

📖 Navigation

Use the hamburger menu (☰) in the top-left corner to switch between sections.

  • Dashboard – Overview of your progress, study time, streaks, and daily goals.
  • Decks – Create, organize, and manage your decks and subdecks.
  • Review – Study your due cards using spaced repetition.
  • Statistics – Detailed analytics for each deck.
  • Help – This page.

📂 Decks & Subdecks

  • Create a deck: Type a name (e.g., Science::Biology) and click Create. The :: creates subdecks.
  • Expand/collapse: Click the ▶/▼ arrow next to a deck.
  • Select a deck: Click the deck name – it highlights and shows options below.
  • View cards: Select a deck, click "View Cards" – the cards appear below. Click "Hide Cards" to collapse them.
  • Import cards: Select a deck, click "Import", choose your .csv or .txt file (format: Question;Answer).
  • Add a card: Select a deck, click "Add Card".
  • Create a subdeck: Select a deck, click the ⊕ (Create Subdeck) button next to the deck name.
  • Delete a deck: Click the 🗑️ icon next to the deck.

🎮 Review Mode

  • Select a deck from the dropdown or click "All Decks".
  • Click "Start Review" – the card front appears at the top.
  • Tap "Show Answer" to reveal the back directly below.
  • Rate your recall: Choose Again, Hard, Good, or Easy at the bottom.
  • Progress: A counter shows 3 / 10 at the top.
  • Timer: A live timer tracks how long you spend reviewing each session.

📊 Statistics

  • Select a deck from the dropdown to see its stats.
  • Retention: % of "Good" or "Easy" ratings in the last 30 days.
  • Exam Readiness: Score out of 100 based on maturity, retention, and coverage.
  • Maturity: New / Learning / Review / Suspended card counts.
  • Due Distribution: Bar chart showing cards due in the next 7 days.
  • Export: Click "Export" to download all cards as Question;Answer.

📈 Dashboard

  • Stat strip: Total cards, due today (with estimated time), retention %, streaks, study time.
  • Study Time: Tracks time spent reviewing today, this week, and this month.
  • Streaks: Two streaks – daily (any review) and goal (meeting your daily goal).
  • Workload Forecast: Cards due in the next 7 days.
  • Weakest Topic: The deck with the lowest retention.
  • Smart Goal: Recommends daily reviews to clear backlog in 7 or 14 days.

🎨 Theme & Settings

  • Dark/Light mode: Toggle with the 🌙/☀ button in the top-right (persists across sessions).
  • Daily Goal: Set in the Statistics tab (Daily Goal section).

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

  • 1 – Again
  • 2 – Hard
  • 3 – Good
  • 4 – Easy
  • Space – Show Answer

❓ Troubleshooting

  • No cards due? Add more cards or wait for intervals to expire.
  • Import not working? Ensure your file is Question;Answer (semicolon only).
  • Review blank? Make sure you have due cards and have selected a deck.
  • Data lost? All data is stored in your browser's IndexedDB – clear at your own risk.
