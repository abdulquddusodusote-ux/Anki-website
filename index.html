Help & User Guide

 Navigation

Use the bottom navigation to switch between sections.

  • Dashboard – Overview of your progress, streaks, study time, and goals.
  • Decks – Create, organize, and manage your decks and subdecks. Tap a deck to start reviewing.
  • Statistics – Detailed analytics for each deck.
  • Help – This page.

 Decks & Subdecks

  • Create a deck: Type a name (e.g., Science::Biology) and click Create.
  • Expand/collapse: Click the / arrow next to a deck.
  • Start reviewing: Single tap any deck to start reviewing its due cards. Or long press and select "Review Cards".
  • Long press (or right-click) on a deck to see: Create Subdeck, Rename, Delete, Import, Review Cards.
  • View cards: Select a deck, click "View Cards".
  • Import cards: Long press a deck  Import.
  • Add a card: Select a deck  Add Card.
  • Delete a deck: Long press a deck  Delete.

 Review Mode

  • Single tap any deck to start reviewing its due cards.
  • The screen becomes full-screen — only the card is visible.
  • Blue = New cards · Red = Learning cards (will come back this session) · Green = Cards due for review.
  • Tap "Show Answer" to reveal the back.
  • Rate your recall: Choose Again, Hard, Good, or Easy.
  • Learning cards (Red) stay in the session and come back automatically when their interval is up.
  • You can leave anytime — your progress is automatically saved.
  • Exit: Tap the back arrow or press Esc.

 Streaks

  • Daily streak: Increases each day you review at least 1 card. Resets if you skip a day.
  • Goal streak: Increases each day you meet your daily card goal. Resets if you miss the goal or skip a day.
  • Streaks are checked when you rate your first card of each new day.

 Keyboard Shortcuts

  • 1 – Again
  • 2 – Hard
  • 3 – Good
  • 4 – Easy
  • Space – Show Answer
  • Esc – Exit review

 Troubleshooting

  • No cards due? Add more cards or wait for intervals to expire.
  • Import not working? Ensure your file is Question;Answer (semicolon only).
  • Review blank? Make sure you have due cards and have selected a deck.
  • Data lost? All data is stored in your browser's IndexedDB.
